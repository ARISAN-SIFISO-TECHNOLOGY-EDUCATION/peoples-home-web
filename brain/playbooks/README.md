## Playbook philosophy

A good playbook:
1. **Is based on what actually happened**, not what should have happened
2. **Includes the gotchas** — the things that went wrong the first time
3. **Links to the relevant brain docs** but doesn't duplicate them
4. **Is updated when the process changes** (append new notes at the bottom)

A playbook is not:
- An architecture document (that's `brain/architecture/`)
- A decision log (that's `brain/decisions/`)
- A vision statement (that's `brain/vision/`)
