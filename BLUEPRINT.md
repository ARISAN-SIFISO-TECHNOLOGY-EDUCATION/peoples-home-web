# THE PEOPLE'S HOME
## National Capability Blueprint — v1

> **Status:** Locked 2026-06-21. The authoritative ecosystem map. Each pillar is a **family of
> apps**, not a single app. This is a multi-year superset: the release machine ships each pillar's
> *Current* app first; the *Additional apps* are the People's Home expansion backlog.

---

## Mission
To provide every South African with a complete pathway to **learn, think, build, earn, and create
opportunity** — regardless of age, income, location, or formal education.

---

# PILLAR 1 — FOUNDATIONS
**Purpose:** Build the essential capabilities every person needs to participate in society.

**Target:** Children · Youth · Adults who missed schooling · Elders seeking literacy or digital inclusion.

**Current apps:**
- Math Adventure RPG
- ReadAfrica

**Additional apps:**

### Everyday English
Learn practical English for daily life.
*Topics:* Reading · Writing · Speaking · Workplace English.

### Everyday Maths
Adult-friendly numeracy.
*Topics:* Money · Percentages · Measurements · Banking · Budgeting.

### Digital Basics
First smartphone and internet skills.
*Topics:* WhatsApp · Email · Online safety · Browsers · Forms.

### Citizen Essentials
Understanding everyday life.
*Topics:* Government services · IDs · SASSA · Municipal services · Rights and responsibilities.

### Languages of South Africa
Local language learning (isiZulu · isiXhosa · Sesotho · English).
*Purpose:* Bridge communication gaps.
