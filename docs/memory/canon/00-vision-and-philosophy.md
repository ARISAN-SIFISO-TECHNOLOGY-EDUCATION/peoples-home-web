# Canon 00 — Vision & Philosophy

> *Start here.* The mission and the worldview that everything else serves. If you read only one
> document, read this one.

---

## The one sentence

> **To provide every South African with a complete pathway to learn, think, build, earn, and
> create opportunity — regardless of age, income, location, or formal education.**

## The one belief

> **Technology should empower society — not gatekeep it.**

This is the moral spine. Every product rule downstream ([`03-product-rules.md`](./03-product-rules.md))
is this belief made concrete: no paywalls (income shouldn't gatekeep), no grades (schooling
shouldn't gatekeep), offline (connectivity shouldn't gatekeep), no accounts/no-data (surveillance
shouldn't gatekeep), SA-first (place shouldn't gatekeep).

---

## Who it's for

Not "students." **People** — across a whole life:
- a **child** beginning with Math Adventure RPG,
- a **young person** learning Python or how to reason,
- a **job seeker** finding opportunities,
- a **founder** building a business,
- an **adult who missed schooling** gaining literacy, numeracy, digital basics,
- an **elder** learning to use a phone.

> **One Home. Many Journeys. One Future.**

## The long-term outcome

A single "home" a person can enter at any age and travel through — *childhood → opportunity* —
without ever hitting a paywall, a grade label, a login wall, or a connection requirement on the
learning side. The learning is free forever; the *opportunity* layer (iKhaya) is where a ready
learner connects to the real economy.

## The philosophy in practice (what makes this different)

1. **Capability, not content.** The unit of value is what a person can *do* afterwards
   (participate, understand, create, judge, build, earn) — not lessons consumed. This drives the
   whole architecture ([`01-capability-architecture.md`](./01-capability-architecture.md)).
2. **Dignity by design.** Ages not grades; progress shown as real-life abilities and first-person
   "I can…" stamps, never percentages or rankings that shame.
3. **Sovereign learning.** Offline, on-device, no data leaves the phone. The learner owns their
   progress (literally — see the [Passport](../platform/peoples-home-passport.md)).
4. **SA-first, not SA-only.** Local names, Rand, languages, contexts — built for South Africa
   first, honestly, rather than a generic global product retrofitted.
5. **Built to last.** Documented (this system), versioned, and architected so a solo developer —
   working with AI — can sustain an institutional-scale vision.
